What Odysseus Did

  1. He was from Ithaca
  2. He was the royal son of King Laertes
  3. He has a son, Telemoceus, prince of Ithaca
  4. He led battalions in the Trojan War
  5. 4. He conceived the Trojan horse stratagem
    6. 5. He was inside the Trojan Horse when it was dragged in through the gates of Troy 6. He joined in the slaughter of Trojans and the destruction of Troy 7. He drove a stake into the Cyclop's eye and blinded him, with the help of his men 8. He told the Cyclops his real name 9. The Cyclops was a son of Poseidon 10. In his fury, the Cyclops beseeched his father to lay waste to Odyseus 11. Odyseus was favored by Athena 12. Zeus gave his brother, Poseidon, permission to harry Odyseus and his crew, destroy their boats, and delay his passage home 13. Odyseus was driven to a rock island and taken care of by the nymph, Calypso 14. Eventually, Athena asked Zeus to order his release 15. Mercury was sent to the island with a message from the high gods: let Odyseus return to his homel at last 16. obediently if Reluctantly, Calypso furnished Odyseus with materials and supplies and advice 17. Odyseus built a sturdy raft 18. Odyseus had a dog named Argos, who was a puppy when he left for Troy 18.5 After his master left, Argos was neglected 18.6 Argos slept on a pile of dung in the town of Ithaca outside the palace of his master 18.7 Odyseus returned to his palace disguised as a begger 19. Argos thumped his tail on seeing his master return, feeble as he was 20. Odyseus recognized Argos and spoke praise for him 21. As Odyseus walked away, Argos died
